The home screen
is the product.

Most apps hide behind an icon you'll stop tapping in a week. Ascent's widgets live on your home screen — your goal, your streak, your motivation battery, right there every time you unlock.

The app is the config layer. The widgets are the system. They turn every unlock from a scroll trigger into a moment of intention.

The Motivation Battery

A real-time readout of your dopamine receptor sensitivity — based on sleep, screen time, exercise, and what you've done today. When the battery drops, Ascent nudges you before you burn out. Not after.

A coach that reads your data

It knows your battery level, your streaks, your Screen Time. When you're struggling, it doesn't say "you got this!" It says "do the 2-minute version and go to bed."

Screen Time, confronted

Ascent pulls your iOS Screen Time data directly. Not to shame you — to make the invisible visible. Hard to keep telling yourself "I don't scroll that much" when the number's right there.

60-day curriculum,
not a to-do list

You set the goal. AI generates a phased plan with weekly milestones. Each morning you get one task — concrete, doable, connected to where you're headed.
