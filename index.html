You don't have a discipline problem.

Your phone was engineered by some of the smartest people alive to capture your attention and not give it back. The gap between unlocking and opening a feed is shorter than conscious thought.

Every habit app you've tried asked you to try harder. That's not a solution — it's the same trap wearing a different outfit. Most get deleted within two weeks because guilt doesn't work as a feature.

Ascent doesn't ask you to try harder. It redesigns what happens when you pick up your phone.

Replace the reflex.

Morning intention → daily practice → evening review. Your goals on your home screen, not buried in an app you forget to open.

The Motivation Battery

A real-time readout of your dopamine receptor sensitivity — based on sleep, screen time, exercise, and what you've done today. When the battery drops, Ascent nudges you before you burn out. Not after.

A coach that reads your data

It knows your battery level, your streaks, your Screen Time. When you're struggling, it doesn't say "you got this!" It says "do the 2-minute version and go to bed." Because that's what actually helps.

Screen Time, confronted

Ascent pulls your iOS Screen Time data directly. Not to shame you — to make the invisible visible. Hard to keep telling yourself "I don't scroll that much" when the number's right there next to your goals.

60-day curriculum, not a to-do list

You set the goal. AI generates a phased plan with weekly milestones that ramp up over time. Each morning you get one task — concrete, doable, connected to where you're headed.
